Shield of Summer's Storms

Armor (Shield), Uncommon (requires attunement)

A small ornate buckler encrusted with the House Summer symbol. When used, yellow electricity expands the shield and can protect the user like a normal shield.

While holding this shield, you gain a bonus to AC as though you were holding a normal shield. On your turn, you can attack with this shield using Strength or Dexterity as if it were a weapon, dealing 1d6 + Proficiency + Attack Mod Lightning damage on a hit. Once per turn, you can throw the Shield as one of your weapon attacks as though it had the thrown property (20/60 and return to your hand after the attack hits.
